Block Producer Code of Conduct

Mission:
Blockchain technology is increasingly inspiring people worldwide. Our mission is to expand the global adoption of cryptocurrency and blockchain solutions, enriching lives and transforming industries.

Pledges:

We commit to being a reliable block producer, operating with unwavering integrity.
We dedicate a portion of our block rewards to supporting blockchain ecosystems and funding dApp development.
We adhere to community guidelines and comply fully with all arbitration rules and mechanisms.
Transparency and openness in our financial activities are the core of our values.
